Understanding the Field

My chosen path: Cybersecurity Analyst — the line of defence between organisations and the threats trying to break in.

🛡️
What does a Cybersecurity Analyst do?

In short — they keep an organisation's systems safe. A Cybersecurity Analyst watches networks for suspicious activity, investigates incidents when something goes wrong, and patches weak points before attackers find them. With cybercrime rising fast across Malaysia's banking, aviation, and healthcare sectors, this is one of the most in-demand jobs in tech today — and exactly where my network security and forensics training has been leading me.
